Frequently Asked Questions

What is Adae?

Adae is a safe, secure, and convenient peer-to-peer platform where users can offer their services to others.

I want to offer a Service, what do I do?

- First, create an account. Remember that having a photo of yourself and validating your email & phone number will make you more credible to your customers.

- Next, create your Service and fill out the title, description, and the price. Be sure to upload a high quality photo that best represents your service!

- If people are interested, they will message you. You will be able to set up a meeting location and discuss the details regarding your service offer.

- When you meet and before you begin the service, ask your customer to scan the in-app QR code that is unique to each transaction on your phone. This will notify our system that the secure transaction has started.

- When your service has ended, display your QR code again and have you customer scan it. This will notify our system that the transaction has ended. This is when you will receive payment as well.

- The In-Scan and Out-Scan is timestamped and proof of your start and end time. This can be used in case of disputes/shortage of payment.

- If the Service charges a Flat Rate, only one scan is needed. Payment will be sent whenver the first scan occurs.

I am looking for something, what do I do?

- First, create an account. Remember that having a photo of yourself will make you more credible to the owners.

- Browse services in your area, look at the item and click "Request". You will need to add a valid credit card before you enter into a transaction, but you will not be charged until you work out all the details, and your offer gets accepted.

- When you meet, open up the transaction on the Adae scanner app, where you will find the QR scanner. Have the owner present their QR code and scan it. This will notify our system that the secure transaction has started.

- When your service has ended, ask the owner/service provider for their QR code and scan it. This will notify our system that the transaction has ended.

I don't see what I want, what can I do?

- Post a Shout Out! On the navbar, locate the "Shout Outs" section. In the drop down menu, select "Post a Shout Out"

How do I cancel my Requests?

Simple! Go to the My Transactions page, and click the cancel button next to all pending items. Note that you will only be able to cancel hourly-items 6 hours in advance, and daily or longer items 24 hours in advance to get your full refund back. If you need to cancel after that, please email us and we will evaluate the situation and may only issue a partial refund.

Is Adae safe and secure?

We do a number of things to ensure that the transaction is safe and secure:

  • All users are validated, and have public profiles where you can see their ratings and reviews.
  • All requests are handled by us, so you don't need to email or text each other, or worry about the other person not showing up.
  • We handle the money part using Stripe, one of the best and most secure payment technology online.
  • In case of disputes, we are always here to try to help you to resolve the issues.

If you have any more questions not covered here, send us a message at info@adae.co, we'd be more than happy to help you! :)
